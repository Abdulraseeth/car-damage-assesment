ABOUT PROJECT


Car Damage Assessment is used to reduce insurance claims leakage during insurance processing.
Visual inspection and validation are usually done. It takes a long time, because an insurance agent is needed to inspect and assess the damaged parts in the vehicle. Here, we are trying to automate the procedure with the help of Artifical Intelligence and Machine Learning. Using this automation, we can avoid time consumption for the insurance claim process and can also prevent claims leakage. This will also be very helpful for car owners and repair shop owners because they can upload the damaged part of the car and can get a rough estimated cost to repair within seconds.
